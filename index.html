About

Dr B B Singh stands out to be one of the innovative writers of the modern era. He was actively engaged in teaching and research at B.Sc. (Ag.), M.Sc. (Ag.) and Ph.D. levels, at N.D. University of Agrilculture and Technology, Faizabad, U.P. Having authored more than two dozen and edited numerous books, he holds a rich experience in the publishing industry; skilled in research, copywriting and copy editing. He is the first recepient of the Young Scientist Award conferred by International Society for Environmental Protection, among other accolades including fellowship of Living World Society (FLWS) and International Society for Environmental Protection (FISEP) towards outstanding contribution in the field of Agricultural Sciences. He has published number of research papers in international journals of repute and also presented various research papers in several International Conferences. Dr. Singh is currently involved writing and editing of books.
